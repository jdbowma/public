How to Convert GIFs to PNG Images


  1. Click the Upload GIF button.

  2. Select your GIF (.gif) file.

  3. Press Convert to PNG.

  4. When the conversion finishes, click the Download as ZIP button to save each frame of your gif in a ZIP archive file. Note that if you do not download your ZIP and then leave the page or convert a new image, it will need to be converted again.

  5. Alternatively, you can right click individual frames and save them to your computer as PNG image files.

    6. For more assistance, refer to our guides, tutorials, and troubleshooting page.


FAQ and Description

Our conversion tool is free, fast, and unlimited. Convert as many files as you want, with no limit on file size or number of conversions.

    FAQ:

  • What is this tool and what does it do?

    • This tool is a free, online converter that will convert a GIF animated image file into multiple PNG images. One PNG will be generated for each frame in your GIF. They are then bundled into a single .zip file for your convenience.

  • Is this tool free?

    • Yes, this tool is completely free.

  • If this is free, how do you make money?

    • Currently, we don't. In the future, we plan to add a few ads that do not affect the user experience. We do not store any user data on our servers.

  • What can I do with my PNGs?

    • It is not the most common file conversion, but sometimes you may want to extract each frame out of a GIF and save them as images. This can be helpful for creating stop motion animations, finding the perfect frame of a video to use as a still photo, or precisely analyzing the frames of a video for any number of purposes.

    In order to get a video into GIF format so that it can be converted into PNGs by our tool, use our free Video to GIF maker.

  • Why should I use this tool over the millions of other free ones out there?

    • Our tool is very fast and completely free, offering no paid plans at this time or limits on conversions or file size. We offer a clean, streamlined experience free of clutter. We do not ask you for your email or to sign up for an account and we do not store your images anywhere. Also, our tool works on your iPhone, iPad and Android mobile devices.

  • Is there a limit to how many GIFs I can convert?

    • No. This tool is unrestricted and allows for unlimited conversions per day. However, you can only upload one GIF at a time.

  • Is there a limit to how large uploaded files can be?

    • No. For now, there is no limit to file sizes. Our converter is designed to run on your computer locally, as opposed to consuming our server's resources. That means the only limit to video-to-GIF conversion on our platform is the processing speed of your computer.

  • Can I save my converted PNGs on your website?

    • No. No files are stored on our server. If you do not download a converted file and then close the tab, you will need to convert it again.

  • Can I convert several GIFs at once?

    • At the moment, conversions must be done one at a time. This is something we are working on improving in the future.

  • How long will it take to convert my GIF into a PNG?

    • That depends on a number of factors including internet speeds, your computer's available resources, your browser, the frame rate, and the length of the GIF.

  • Can I convert GIFs to videos with this tool?

    • At the moment, no. In the future, we plan to release a variety of other free, browser-based converters, including a GIF-to-video converter. Stay tuned!
