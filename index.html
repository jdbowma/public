How to Convert Videos to GIFs


  1. Click the Upload Video button.

  2. Select your .mp4 or .mov video file.

  3. Configure the frame rate. Usually around 10-15 is a safe bet. Values above 15 frames per second are prone to longer conversion times.

  4. Set the quality. Usually "Low Quality" provides the fastest conversion times.

  5. Press Convert to GIF and observe the real-time progress bar as it updates you on the progress of your conversion.

  6. When the conversion finishes, click the Download Image button to save your image. Note that if you do not download your GIF and then leave the page or convert a new image, it will need to be converted again.

  7. Enjoy your GIF!

  • What can I do with my GIF?

    • GIFs are image files that can be played as an animation, similar to a video. They are useful for sharing in situations where images are more appropriate than videos, such as in PowerPoint presentations, posts on social media sites like X or Facebook, or in text messages and emails. They often loop repeatedly and feature a signature "choppy" feel, although they can be customized to be as smooth and high quality as videos.

    For more information about using GIFs in PowerPoint presentations, refer to the Microsoft Support article on the topic.
